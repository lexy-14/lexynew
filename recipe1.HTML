Perfect Kenyan Chapati

Prep: 30 mins Cook: 20 mins Makes: 8

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup ghee or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add water gradually while kneading
  3. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth
  4. Divide dough into 8 balls, coat with oil
  5. Rest covered for 30 minutes
  6. Roll each ball into thin circles
  7. Brush with ghee, fold, and roll again
  8. Cook on medium heat until golden

Serving Suggestions

Perfect with stews | Great for wraps | Kenyan staple
