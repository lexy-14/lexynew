Fragrant Kenyan Pilau

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 40 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef, cubed
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes
  2. Heat oil, fry onions until golden brown
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and spices
  4. Add beef and brown on all sides
  5. Pour in beef stock and bring to boil
  6. Add drained rice, stir gently
  7. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes
  8. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving

Serving Tradition

Serve with kachumbari | Perfect for gatherings | Celebration dish
